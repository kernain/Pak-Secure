Pak Secure wants to ensure you reach safely to your loved one!

Expats and foreign returns are easy targets for criminals! Your safety, being utmost crucial, we have selected a trusted partner to provide an armed Patrol vehicle that will escort the passenger car with luggage from/to the airport to/from your place of residence.

Our partner, Pak Security Services has been providing security services to households and corporates all over Pakistan for over three decades. Their armed personnel are selected after clearing several vigorous vetting and selection processes. The security staff is monitored by a team of experienced field supervisors, many of whom have backgrounds in the military and law enforcement; ensuring all of your security needs are met on the job. Providing you a Peace of mind!

We will reach out to your local contact to ensure smooth coordination with your family/friends receiving you at the airport. If you don’t have anyone receiving you, no problem, we can also arrange a passenger car with a driver for you!
